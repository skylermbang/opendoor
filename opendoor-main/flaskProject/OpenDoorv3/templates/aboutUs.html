ABOUT US

Open door is an online creative education platform that helps students develop business ideas.

Thinking of creative ideas business ideas can be a really strenuous, exhausting, and difficult process. But we believe that it should not be.

We want to make the creative thinking of remarkable business ideas a beautiful and enjoyable process. We want your ideas to see the light of day and address the problems our societies and communities face.

We aim to build a community of creative thinkers, future entrepreneurs, doers who want to turn their passions with purpose into professions that help change the world.
