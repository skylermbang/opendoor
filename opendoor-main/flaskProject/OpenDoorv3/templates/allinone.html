Step 1

The customer is your target market for whom you should brainstorm possible problems and solutions This step requires you to identify as many realistic problems as you can for the customer you have been assigned

Step 2

With your assigned customer you will be provided with a hint for a solution that you can use This step requires you to identify as many realistic solutions with the hints for the customer assigned to you

Step 3

After brainstorming your problems and solutions you can narrow it down into a mind map provided for you This step also enables you to improve your ideation and prepares you to effectually adopt the business model canvas for subsequent business planning
